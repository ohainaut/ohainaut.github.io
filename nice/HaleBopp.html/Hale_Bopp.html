Comet Hale-Bopp

In this page, you will find a few images of Comet Hale-Bopp that I took during my past observing runs; it does not have the pretention to be an exhaustive source on information on that comet; if you want to learn EVERYTHING about Comet Hale-Bopp, go to Russel Sipes' site (with contributions from Hale and Bopp, and from Carolyn Collins Petersen, whose nice virtual planetarium is mirrored on this site), or the extremely well documented ESO Hale-Bopp page. You will also find some images and info on the Institute for Astronomy Hale-Bopp page.

  • November 1996, on the 2.2m telescope of the University of Hawai`i at Mauna Kea.
  • September 1996, on the 3.6m Canada-France-Hawai`i telescope, also on Mauna Kea, equipped with the largest CCD mosaic in the world (no false modesty: this image is superb).
  • June 1996 on the UH 2.2m telescope on Mauna Kea, with the 8k CCD mosaic.
  • Mars 1996, on the New Technology Telescope at the European Southern Observatory, La Silla (Chile).
  • "Hale Bopp's Mysterious companion?" or, "how a SAO star can start a UFO story". Updated for the new companions.
