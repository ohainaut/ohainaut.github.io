To introduce myself shortly, I was born in Belgium in 1965, raised in a wonderful family who rebuilt the world every week until dawn with friends, when they were not in search of the Graal. Although always strongly attracted by artistics studies (drawing and painting) and enclined to listen first to my subjective side, I chose the dry path and got a master in Physics; married an astronomer, whom I have been following from one observatory to another for the past 8 years (sighh...), constantly adjusting to the place and situation, and doing many different things from dental assistant to observing assistant,my last - and very exciting! - activity.
